# Security Policy for BreatheEasy

## Supported Versions

We are committed to ensuring the security of the latest version of BreatheEasy on the `main` branch.

| Version | Supported          |
| ------- | ------------------ |
| 1.0.0   | :white_check_mark: |

## Reporting a Vulnerability

The BreatheEasy team and community take all security vulnerabilities seriously. We appreciate your efforts to responsibly disclose your findings, and will make every effort to acknowledge your contributions.

To report a security vulnerability, please send an email to **[YOUR_EMAIL_ADDRESS_HERE]** with "SECURITY" in the subject line.

Please include the following information in your report:
- A clear description of the vulnerability.
- Steps to reproduce it.
- Your assessment of the impact of the vulnerability.

We will acknowledge your email within 48 hours and will send a more detailed response within 72 hours indicating the next steps in handling your report. We ask that you do not disclose the vulnerability publicly until we have had a chance to address it.

Thank you for helping to keep BreatheEasy secure.
